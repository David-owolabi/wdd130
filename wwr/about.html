History

Founded in 2005 by a group of adventure enthusiasts, RapidQuest Rafting began as a small team guiding weekend trips along the Clearwater River. Over the years, our passion for the outdoors and commitment to safety have helped us grow into one of the region’s most trusted names in whitewater adventures. From humble beginnings to thrilling expeditions, we’ve been riding the waves ever since.
